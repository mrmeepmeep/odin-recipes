Pizza

Description

This beatiful cheesy pizza is delicious and more important very easy to make! Especially after a long day at work you crave a filled pizza filled with toppings

This pizza consists mostly out of cheese almost 90% is just cheese! The other 10 percent is the dough. Thats it only cheese and dough nothing else!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • More cheese
  • Little bit of dough

Steps

  • Buy cheese and dough
  • Flatten the dough into a circle
  • Place the cheese on top
  • Put it in the oven at 220 degrees celsius
  • cook 30 minutes
  • Ta-da! Time to eat your pizza with 90% cheese!
